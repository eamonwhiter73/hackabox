Hackabox will be the first open-source, "hack as you wish," game development platform.


I am trying to create an open-source gaming platform, that also comes packaged with all the tools needed to develop for it. I want to also create a service that anyone can upload content to. The system will be based on Raspberry Pi. Obviously, it's graphics capabilities will not be as good as your standard gaming platform, but that isn't the point. Games like Minecraft have proven that you don't need resource intensive graphics to make a great game...imagine developing cool 2D games (and maybe some 3D games), on your TV...and then uploading it to a database where you could sell it, or give it away!

What about Steam Machine, you say? Steam Machine, as it was released, has an extremely stripped down version of Linux for its OS - I do not plan on stripping away everything...I wan't anyone to be able to modify the Hackabox and create games.

For the parents: Hackabox would finally incorporate aspects of creativity, and learning, with the time that your child spends in front of the TV, playing games. If you have a tech savvy kid, foster their needs by getting them a Hackabox! With a Hackabox, your kid will be able to create his own games...which can be played by the whole family!
